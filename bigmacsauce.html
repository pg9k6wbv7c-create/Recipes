Big Mac Sauce Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish
  • 1 tablespoon finely minced onion
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 teaspoons yellow mustard
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Place all ingredients in bowl and stir to combine. Can be used immediately but for best results cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving. Will keep for 2 weeks cold. Makes approximately 3/4 cup of sauce.
