Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 4-6 ripe avocados (the softer the better)
  • 1/2 cup pico de gallo (see recipe)
  • 1 lime
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Peel avocados and mash them in a large bowl.
  2. Add pico de gallo and lime juice, then season with salt and pepper.
  3. Mix well to combine all ingredients.
