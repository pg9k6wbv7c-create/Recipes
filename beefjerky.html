Beef Jerky Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2-3 pounds sirloin tip roast
  • 1/2 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon liquid smoke
  • 2 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon mustard seed

Instructions:

  1. Slice meat cross-grain into 1/8" thick slices.
  2. Mix all other ingredients in a saucepan.
  3. Warm on low heat while stirring until sugars are dissolved. Do not overheat or boil!
  4. Spoon some of the mixture into the bottom of a large marinating dish.
  5. Layer in the meat, spooning the marinade over each layer. Marinate 24-48 hours in the refrigerator
  6. Preheat oven to 150-175Â°F.
  7. Remove meat from marinade and pat dry with paper towels.
  8. Place a wire rack on a baking sheet. Lay the meat strips across the rack in a single layer.
  9. Place a wooden spoon in the oven door to allow moisture to escape during drying. Drying time will vary depending on thickness, how crowded the oven is, and personal preferences, but usually 3-4 hours is sufficient. Store in refrigerator for up to 2 weeks or room temp for a few days.
