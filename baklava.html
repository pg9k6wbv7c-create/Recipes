Baklava

Ingredients

  • 1 package phyllo dough
  • 1 pound chopped nuts (walnuts, pistachios, or a mix)
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Prepare the syrup. In a saucepan, combine the water and sugar. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for about 10 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in honey and vanilla extract. Place in refrigerator to cool while you prepare the baklava.
  2. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  3. In a bowl, combine the chopped nuts and ground cinnamon.
  4. Brush a 9x13 inch baking dish with melted butter. Layer 8 sheets of phyllo dough in the dish, brushing each sheet with butter before adding the next.
  5. Sprinkle a thin layer of the nut mixture over the phyllo layers.
  6. Continue layering phyllo sheets and nut mixture, brushing each sheet with butter, until all the nuts are used. Finish with a final layer of about 8 phyllo sheets.
  7. Using a sharp knife, cut the baklava into diamond or square shapes.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for about 45-50 minutes, or until the baklava is golden brown and crisp.
  9. Once the baklava is done baking, immediately pour the cold syrup over it, ensuring it seeps into all the cuts and layers.
  10. Allow the baklava to cool completely before serving. This allows the syrup to soak in and the flavors to meld together.
