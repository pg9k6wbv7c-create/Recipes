Caramels

Ingredients

  • 3 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups light corn syrup
  • 3 pints whipping cream
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large, COLD dutch oven or stock pot, mix together the sugar, corn syrup, and 1 pint whipping cream. Bring to a "soft ball".
  2. Add 2nd pint of whipping cream. Bring to a "soft ball".
  3. Add the last pint of whipping cream. Bring to 242 degrees.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract.
  5. Pour into 9x13 pan lined with parchment paper. Let cool until firm.
  6. Cut into small squares. Wrap each square in wax paper or cellophane.

Notes:1) Stir constantly while on heat to prevent burning. 2)Use a candy thermometer for accurate temperature readings.
