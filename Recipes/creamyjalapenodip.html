Creamy Jalapeño Dip

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 3/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 12 ounce jar jalapenos, drain and reserve liquid
  • 1/4 cup tomatillo sauce
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 packet Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Dressing Mix
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients and 2 tablespoons of the reserved jalapeño liquid to a blender and pulse until desired smoothness.
  2. Adjust consistency with additional reserved jalapeño liquid, if needed.
  3. Transfer to a serving bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.
  4. Serve with tortilla chips or vegetables.
  5. Enjoy!
