Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 whole rotisserie chicken, cooked, cooled, pulled off the bone and chopped
  • 1-1 1/2 cups mayonnaise, depending on size of chicken
  • 1 teaspoon yellow mustard
  • 1/2 cup sweet pickle relish
  • 1/4 cup sweet pickle juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, season chicken with salt and pepper.
  2. Add sweet pickle juice and stir to combine well.
  3. Add sweet pickle relish and mix well.
  4. Add remaining ingredients and mix well.
  5. Season with additional salt and pepper to taste.

Notes: 1) Adjust mayonnaise amount based on desired creaminess. 2) Chill before serving for best flavor. 3) Add fruit or nuts if desired. 4) Great as a dip!
